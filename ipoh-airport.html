Ipoh (IPH)

Ipoh (IPH), the gateway to the Ipoh, is a premier aviation hub offering modern facilities and unparalleled services. Conveniently located on Hulhulé Island near the capital, Malé, IPH connects travellers to a network of domestic and international destinations. Whether visiting for leisure or business, the flight from Ipoh airport ensures a smooth and memorable travel experience.

Facilities at Ipoh (IPH)

Ipoh is equipped with various facilities to maximise passenger comfort and convenience:

  1. Modern Terminals: Separate terminals cater to domestic and international flights, ensuring efficient navigation for travellers.
  2. Shopping: Explore duty-free outlets featuring a selection of international and local brands.
  3. Dining: Enjoy a variety of dining options, from quick snacks to restaurants serving international and Maldivian cuisine.
  4. Business Services: Business lounges and meeting spaces offer a comfortable environment for work or relaxation.
  5. Free Wi-Fi: Complimentary high-speed Wi-Fi is available throughout the airport to keep passengers connected.
  6. Currency Exchange & ATMs: Multiple currency exchange counters and ATMs are conveniently located across the airport.
  7. Lost & Found: A dedicated service assists passengers in recovering misplaced items.

Important Information for Passengers

  • Check-In Times: Arrive 2-3 hours before domestic flights and 3-4 hours before international flights to complete check-in, security, and immigration.
  • Baggage Policy: Check your airline’s baggage policy for restrictions on size and weight to avoid additional charges.Travel Documents: Ensure your boarding pass, passport, and required visas are readily accessible.
  • Security Procedures: Prepare for screening by removing electronic devices and adhering to liquid restrictions.
  • Lost & Found: For assistance with misplaced items, contact the Lost & Found department or visit the dedicated counter.

Travel Tips for Ipoh (IPH)

  1. Arrive Early: Allow extra time to navigate the airport and complete formalities.
  2. Stay Hydrated: Use water stations or purchase bottled water to stay refreshed in the tropical climate.
  3. Check Flight Status: Stay updated on flight schedules via airport displays or online platforms.
  4. Utilise Lounges: Relax in premium lounges to enhance comfort, especially during layovers.
  5. Stay Connected: Use free Wi-Fi to coordinate travel plans and check for updates.

FAQs on Ipoh (IPH)

  1. What are the check-in procedures at IPH airport?
    Check-in typically begins 2-3 hours before domestic flights and 3-4 hours before international flights. Passengers must carry their boarding pass, passport, and any required visas.
  2. Is free Wi-Fi available at Ipoh?
    Yes, complimentary high-speed Wi-Fi is available throughout the airport.
  3. What are the baggage allowances at IPH airport?
    Baggage policies vary by airline. Hand luggage and checked baggage allowances depend on the carrier’s specific guidelines.
  4. How do I go through security checks at IPH airport?
    Security screenings are mandatory. Be prepared to remove electronic devices and comply with liquid restrictions.
  5. What if I lose an item at Ipoh?
    Visit the Lost & Found counter or contact the airport’s support team to recover lost belongings.
